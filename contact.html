Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Change Password. You'll receive an email with reset instructions. For security, we use two-factor authentication.
Is my health data secure?
Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) and never share your data without explicit consent. Your privacy is our top priority.
How does the gamification work?
You earn XP for completing health tasks, maintaining streaks, and achieving goals. Level up to unlock badges and rewards!
Can I share my progress with my doctor?
Yes! Go to Progress ‚Üí Share ‚Üí Send to Doctor. You can export ABDM-compatible reports for seamless sharing with healthcare providers.
What devices are supported?
COMPASS works on all modern smartphones, tablets, and computers. We also integrate with popular health devices and wearables.
